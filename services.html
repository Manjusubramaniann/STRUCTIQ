Rebar Placing Drawings

Our Rebar Placing Drawings are meticulously prepared by highly experienced Civil Engineers, ensuring precision and compliance with ACI and CRSI standards. Using Rebar CAD, we deliver high-quality shop drawings in PDF and DWG formats for seamless project integration.

Fabrication & Placer Friendly

All unconfined bars are organized into logical groups to minimize bar marks and enhance clarity. Our placer-friendly layouts support efficient fabrication, cost-effective placement, and timely project completion.

Clear & Easy to Understand

Each drawing sheet includes a unique bar bending schedule for easy interpretation. Detailed cross-sections and enlarged views are provided for congested reinforcement areas to eliminate ambiguity.
