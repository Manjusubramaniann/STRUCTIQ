Rebar Placing Drawings

Our rebar placing drawings are developed by experienced civil engineers with a strong focus on precision and compliance with ACI and CRSI standards. Leveraging Rebar CAD, we produce high-quality shop drawings in PDF and DWG formats to support seamless construction workflows.

Fabrication & Placer Friendly

Unconfined bars are systematically grouped to reduce bar marks and improve clarity, with placer-friendly layouts that enable efficient fabrication, cost-effective placement, and on-time project delivery.

Clear & Easy to Understand

Each drawing sheet is accompanied by a dedicated bar bending schedule to ensure clear interpretation. Detailed cross-sections and enlarged views are provided for congested reinforcement areas, eliminating ambiguity during fabrication and placement.
