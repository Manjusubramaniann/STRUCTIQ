Who We Are

We deliver global rebar detailing and estimation services for reinforced concrete structures, ensuring accurate, constructible, and fully coordinated reinforcement solutions aligned with international standards.

We partner with contractors, consultants, and developers across the globe to produce high-quality rebar detailing outputs that meet project specifications, international codes, and tight delivery schedules.

Our Expertise

Accurate rebar detailing is fundamental to construction success. Our expertise reduces site conflicts, minimizes rework, and mitigates costly project delays.

Leveraging industry-approved software including REBAR CAD and CADS RC, we produce precise reinforcement drawings and bar bending schedules optimized for standard construction workflows.

Professional Commitment

We are committed to quality, accountability, and client confidence, executing every project with exceptional attention to detail, coordination, and quality assurance.

We securely deliver all project outputs via email or FTP in DWG, DWF, PDF, and XLS formats, enabling seamless collaboration across global project teams.
