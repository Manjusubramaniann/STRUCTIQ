About Us

Delivering precision-driven rebar detailing and estimation services for reinforced concrete structures worldwide.

Who We Are

We provide global rebar detailing and estimation services for reinforced concrete structures, delivering precise, constructible, and fully coordinated reinforcement solutions.

Our team partners with contractors, consultants, and developers worldwide to produce detailing outputs that comply with project specifications, international codes, and demanding schedules.

Our Expertise

Precision in rebar detailing is essential to successful construction. Our expertise helps prevent site conflicts, minimize rework, and avoid costly project delays.

Using industry-approved software such as REBAR CAD and CADS RC, we deliver accurate reinforcement drawings and bar bending schedules formatted for direct use in standard construction workflows.

Professional Commitment

Our team is driven by quality, accountability, and client confidence. Every project is executed with meticulous attention to detail, coordination, and quality control.

All deliverables are securely issued via email or FTP in DWG, DWF, PDF, and XLS formats, ensuring seamless collaboration across international project teams.
