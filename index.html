Accuracy That Drives Construction

We provide global rebar detailing and estimation services for reinforced concrete structures, delivering precise, constructible, and fully coordinated reinforcement solutions.

Our team is driven by quality, accountability, and client confidence. We partner with contractors, consultants, and developers worldwide to produce detailing outputs that comply with project specifications, international codes, and demanding schedules. Precision in detailing is essential to successful construction, and our expertise helps prevent site conflicts, rework, and costly delays.

Using advanced, industry-approved software such as REBAR CAD and CADS RC, we produce accurate reinforcement drawings and bar bending schedules, with data formatted for direct use in XLS and other standard formats.

All deliverables are securely issued via email or FTP in DWG, DWF, PDF, and XLS formats, ensuring seamless coordination across international project teams.
